The official step by step guide to becoming Barbie

The first step to becoming Barbie would be getting her infamous Barbie Dreamhouse. Of course, to do that, you would need a lot of money, which brings us to the next step.
Step 2: Barbie has a different job everyday, so to become her, you will have to do the same!

Job suggestions for each day of the week:
- Firefighter

- CEO

- Diver

- Environmental Activist

- Teacher

- Cook

- On sundays, Barbie relaxes

These are only a few suggestions, you can do whatever you want. Of course, to become the best Barbie version of yourself, you should find a job for each day of the year!
To become a Barbie, there is no specific look you should go for, the only rule is that you must always wear pink. The Barbiecore aesthetic is the most crucial step of becoming Barbie.
The final step to becoming Barbie is having a boyfriend, preferably named Ken with a great personality and a beach job.

There you go! A step by step guide to becoming Barbie. Good luck to all the girlies out there! And remember, life in plastic, it's fantastic.
